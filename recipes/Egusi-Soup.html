Egusi Soup Recipe

Description

Egusi soup is native to West Africa, Nigeria in particular. It is a great soup.

Ingredients

    A quarter cup pf pumpkin seeds, one and a half pounds cubed beef stew meat half cup of peanut oil and salt to taste 2 large tomatoes and 1 small onion, chopped ground habanero pepper, one and a half cup of water 3 tablespoons tomato paste and 1 pound fresh spinach, washed and chopped

Steps to prepare Egusi Soup

h4>
    Place pumpkin seeds in a blender, blend until mixture is powdery, 30 to 40 seconds Cut beef into bite-sized cubes, season with salt Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook beef in hot oil until brown but not cooked through, 3 to 5 minutes Place tomatoes, onion, and peppers in a blender, blend until smooth, about 30 seconds. Stir tomato mixture into beef, reduce heat to medium-low and cover. Cook until meat is tender, 40 to 50 minutes Add tomato sauce, water, and tomato paste, simmer for 10 minutes Stir in spinach and powdered pumpkin seeds. Continue simmering for 10 more minutes.
